# Lynn Taff (Sister) History & Health Profile

**Source:** Email from Lynn to Will (July 13, 2022)
**Context:** Timeline of health decline (POTS) and family rupture/reconciliation.

## Health Timeline (POTS Diagnosis)
*   **2010:** First tachycardia episode (after eating giant burrito). Working at hospice. Running/yoga active. Checked by **Dr. Ness**, dismissed. Quit job for "fatigue." Moved to Asheville via Seattle/Vermont.
*   **2013:** Hospitalization/Stress Test. Discharged with "acute stress" and "GERD." Tech noted heart rate rise on standing (POTS indicator), but physician ignored it. Beginning of "dark times" (undiagnosed struggle).
*   **2018 (July):** Hospitalized for 4 days. **Tilt Table Test** performed (gold standard for POTS).
*   **2018 (Sept 13):** Cardiology follow-up. **OFFICIAL DIAGNOSIS:** **POTS (Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome).**
*   **Impact:** "Lost at least five years of my life." Caused havoc in marriage, work, and finances.

## Family Relations Timeline
*   **2014:** Family friction regarding Rhett's will. Lynn asserts this was NOT the cause of her silence/rupture. Continued communication with Will during this time.
*   **2017 (March 20):** **The Rupture Event.**
    *   **Incident:** Connie fired a gun in the house (Baker Act).
    *   **Conflict:** Lynn called to talk to Rhett while Connie was in psych hospital. Later called again, Connie refused to put Rhett on phone. Lynn lost temper (only time they butted heads). Connie accused Lynn of calling "about the will."
    *   **Result:** Lynn went quiet due to the chaos of the incident and family gossip framing it as being about the will.
*   **2018:** **Reconciliation.**
    *   Impetus: Laura's cancer treatment. Lynn was heavily in touch with Laura.
    *   Will reached out asking if Lynn wanted contact again. (She did).
    *   Josh and Lynn moved to Vermont (Fall 2018).
