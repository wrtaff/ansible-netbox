## Church Conventions & Terminology
- **Brother / Sister:** In the church, brethren are referred to by these titles even if not blood kin.
- **Elder:** Used in titles for pastors (e.g., Elder Win Kuck) instead of "Brother" to distinguish their role.
- **Common Misspellings to Watch:**
  - **Lelo** is often misspelled as **Lilo**.
  - **Totty** is often misspelled as **Toddie** or **Tottie**.
  - **Taff** is sometimes misspelled as **Taft**.
  - **Kuck** is sometimes misspelled as **Cook**.
  - **Chelsey** is often misspelled as **Chelsea**.
  - **Allene** is sometimes transcribed as **Arlene** or **Leeny**.
