I, WILLIAM R. TAFF, SR. ("Rhett"), presently residing at 3778 Crawfordville Highway, Crawfordville, Florida, 32327, hereby make the following declaration naming Preneed Guardians to serve in the event of my future incapacity: 

If I am at any time determined to be an incapacitated person, as that term is defined in the Florida Guardianship Law as it now exists or may hereafter be amended, I declare that my son, William R. Taff, Jr. ("Will"), whose present address is 296 Pine Lane, Crawfordville, Florida 32327, is to serve as Plenary Guardian of the property, to exercise all delegable legal rights and powers and to perform all tasks necessary to care for my property or estate. Taff ("Laura"), whose present address is 12 Card Lane, Crawfordville, Florida 32327, to serve as Plenary Guardian of the property, to exercise all delegable legal rights and powers and to perform all tasks necessary to care for my property or estate.

In the event my son, Will is unable to act as such I designate my daughter, Laura, to serve as Plenary Guardian of the person, to exercise all delegable legal rights and powers and to perform all the tasks necessary to care for my person. 

I further declare that it is my intent and desire that the above\-named persons be appointed by the Court having jurisdiction to serve in their respective capacities without bond.
