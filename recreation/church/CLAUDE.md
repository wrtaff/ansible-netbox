## Church Identity
- **Full name:** Liberty Primitive Baptist Church
- **Location:** Smithville, Georgia (Webster County) — associated with Preston, GA area but physically in Smithville
- **Affiliation:** Primitive Baptist (Calvinist/predestinarian; no Sunday school, no missionary society)
- **Services:** Every Sunday, 10:30 AM
- **Website:** http://libertypbc.org

## Terminology
- Members address one another as **Brother** or **Sister** (not necessarily blood kin)
- Pastors are addressed as **Elder** (e.g., "Elder Win Kuck"), not "Brother"
- **Conference** = monthly business meeting (held 2nd Sunday)
- **Annual Meeting** = multi-day worship event beginning Friday 6 PM before the 3rd Sunday in June; Communion (Lord's Supper) on Saturday 6 PM
- **Gospel Dealings** = member welfare / prayer requests section of the conference agenda
- **Moderator** = presides over the conference (usually the pastor; if absent, church elects one)
