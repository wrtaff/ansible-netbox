# 2026 Model Train Layout Plan: E&P Mill 3

**Date:** January 26, 2026
**Project Status:** Planning / Initial Breakdown
**Context:** Documentation of January 10th planning session (Will & Jen Taff)

## 1. Physical Infrastructure & Furniture Protection
*   **Media Cabinets:** Must be protected during the build.
*   **Baseboard (Northern Village):** Single piece of plywood cut to fit the entire area, including inserts that extend to the drywall.
*   **Padding:** Felt bottom on all plywood/foam bases sitting on media cabinets.
*   **Support:** Sawhorses to be used for temporary support/elevation.

## 2. Western Town (The "Circle" Layout)
*   **Tabletop Dimensions:** 43" x 45"
*   **Track Compatibility:** Must accommodate a **42" diameter circle** for the passenger train.
*   **Split Design:** Tabletop should be split/modular to allow passenger train access across the layout.

## 3. Topography & Levels
A three-level "stepped" design for the town:
*   **Level 1 (West/TV Side):** Highest elevation (approx. 3-4 inches using stacked foam).
*   **Level 2 (Middle):** Intermediate height.
*   **Level 3 (Track Level):** Lowest point, level with the bottom board.
*   **Town Square:** Expected to be "tiny" due to space taken by track radius and levels.

## 4. Track & Ground Level
*   **Ground Level Radius:** 22-inch radius.
*   **Switch Placement:** Located toward the back of the layout.
*   **Tunneling:** Track must cut under the higher village levels (requires approx. 3" clearance).
*   **Safety/Aesthetics:** "Ditch" design with a fence to prevent falls where the track runs low.
