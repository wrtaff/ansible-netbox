# Eagle & Phenix Social Committee (EPSC) - Master Planner & Agenda

This document serves as the top-level repository for the EPSC. It centralizes information regarding committee leadership, resident engagement, event planning, and social projects.

## 1. Overview & Purpose
The EPSC aims to foster community spirit, rebuild trust (post-COVID), and provide positive outlets for resident engagement. The committee focuses on integrating new residents, managing community communications, and organizing diverse social programming to move building discourse beyond "Facebook complaints."
