## 4. Budget Review (2026 Budget)
*   **Overview:** The primary focus of the meeting was the review and finalization of the 2026 budget.
*   **Highlights:**
    *   **Property Owners Association:** No increase in fees.
    *   **Parking Condominium:** Minimal increase (~$1.75/month or $21/year per space).
    *   **Mill 3 Residential:**
        *   **Fees:** Operating budget increased by $18,361; Reserve contribution increased by $2,305.
        *   **Utilities:** Electricity budget increased by 2% (~$9,000 total increase). Georgia Power rates are holding steady, but the increase covers potential surcharges.
        *   **Staffing:** 3% wage increase approved for the 5 onsite staff members.
        *   **Insurance:** Property and Flood insurance premiums were budgeted with a 10% increase, though recent actuals came in lower than the previous budget.
        *   **Reserves:** Inflation assumption for reserves was adjusted to 3.5% (down from 8.5% used during peak inflation years, but higher than pre-COVID 2%). Guy (Treasurer) expressed comfort with this level.
    *   **Commercial vs. Residential Split:** Discussed the fee structure (~80/20 split generally, but specific items vary). Commercial fees generally increase less than residential due to usage factors (e.g., elevators).
*   **Approval/Next Steps:** The board expressed comfort with the presented budget.
    *   **Action:** The budget packet needs to be finalized and distributed to homeowners by **November 17th** (21 days prior to the annual meeting).
    *   **Action:** Delaney/Bill to send the "Accomplishments and Goals" draft to Will/Elaine for editing by tomorrow (Nov 11).
