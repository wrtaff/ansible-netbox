## 4. Technical Infrastructure & Systems

### A. Security & Cameras (Digital Watchdog)
*   **Current State:** System is functional but underutilized. Permissions issues exist (attendants accidentally moving cameras).
*   **Upgrades:**
    *   Jason Gamache is testing "AI" features on a separate system (Mill 1/Commercial) to identify capabilities (e.g., "door open" alerts, vehicle tracking).
    *   **Bay Deck/Parking:** Desire to install a camera monitoring the South Deck/Parking area.
    *   **Grant Funding:** Potential to recover ~\$5,000 in costs if camera access is shared with the Police "Real Time Crime Center."
*   **Liability:** Discussion on data retention policies (cloud vs. server, 7-day vs. 30-day). Aimee to draft a privacy/retention policy to limit liability.

### B. HVAC (AAON Prism) - **#1 Priority for 2026**
*   **Issue:** The "AAON" rooftop units are inefficient ("bleeding money") and erratic (heating and cooling simultaneously). Hallway temperatures vary wildly (e.g., 5th floor hot, 2nd floor cold).
*   **Current Control:** Requires physical access to the roof and plugging in a laptop.
*   **Solution:** Implement **AAON Prism** software for remote monitoring and control.
*   **Implementation Plan:**
    *   Need to bridge the connection from the roof units to the building network.
    *   *Investigation:* Check existing thermostat wiring in the attendant breakroom to see if it can be repurposed for a data connection.
    *   *Alternative:* Use a wireless bridge (point-to-point) from the roof to the parking deck or lobby Wi-Fi.
    *   *Monitoring:* Consider installing wireless thermostats in hallways to trigger alerts when temperatures drift out of range.

### C. Access Control & Fire Safety
*   **Fire Egress (Critical):** Lobby and hallway doors are *not* unlocking automatically during fire alarms, potentially trapping residents.
    *   *Action:* This must be fixed immediately.
    *   *Upgrade:* Propose installing "Push to Exit" buttons or adjusting the "Rex" (Request to Exit) motion sensors in the lobby to prevent false locks/unlocks.
*   **System ID:** Need to identify the specific software/vendor used for the key fobs to better manage access.
