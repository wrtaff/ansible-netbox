# Liberty Primitive Baptist Church Conference Minutes
**Date:** March 8, 2026
**Moderator:** Elder Win Kuck
**Acting Clerk:** Will Taff

## 1. Call to Order
The conference was called to order by Elder Win Kuck.

## 2. Reading and Approval of Minutes
Brother Jason Blankenship summarized the minutes for the previous three months due to various absences and sicknesses preventing formal conferences during those times.

### December 14, 2025
*   **Gospel Dealings:** Brothers Phillip and Craig Totty reported that Sister Liz Totty had fallen and injured her back. A "Church Sing" had been planned at her home to encourage her, but she was subsequently hospitalized and moved to Magnolia Manor Nursing Care.

### January 25, 2026
*   **New Business:** The church voted to authorize the deacons to open a second checking account at **Citizens Bank of Americus** to improve banking convenience for the church.

### February 8, 2026
*   **New Business:** The church reported the installation of an **RV power port** on the church grounds. This was installed to accommodate traveling visitors.

### Approval
*   **Motion:** Will Taff moved to accept the minutes as read.
*   **Second:** Properly seconded.
*   **Result:** Approved by unanimous vote.

## 3. Acknowledgements
* Elder Win Kuck asked if there were any acknowledgements and there were none. 

## 4. Old Business
*   Brother Jason Blankenship reported that all previous business items were "buttoned up" and no old business remained outstanding.

## 5. New Business
*   **Clerk Transition:**
    *   **Resignation:** Brother George Irwin formally resigned as Church Clerk due to his health and lack of attendance. He requested the church's continued prayers for himself and Sister Eloise.
    *   **Appointment:** Brother George Irwin moved that **Brother Will Taff** be appointed as the new Church Clerk, noting his interest and aptitude for the role.
    *   **Second:** Properly seconded.
    *   **Result:** Approved by unanimous vote. Will Taff is now the official Church Clerk.

## 6. Gospel Dealings
*   None reported beyond the updates mentioned in the historical minutes.

## 7. Closing
*   **Closing Prayer:** Brother Paul Blankenship.
*   **Adjournment:** The conference was adjourned following the prayer.

***
*Minutes recorded by Will Taff, Clerk.*
